# 06-itil-framework.md

## 1. ITIL Service Value System
*   **Guiding Principles**: 
    *   Focus on value
    *   Start where you are
    *   Progress iteratively with feedback
    *   Collaborate and promote visibility
    *   Think and work holistically
    *   Keep it simple and practical
    *   Optimize and automate
*   **Governance**: Ensure continual alignment with organizational strategy
*   **Service Value Chain**: Plan, Improve, Engage, Design and Transition, Obtain/Build, Deliver and Support
*   **Practices**: 34 ITIL practices organized into categories

## 2. ITIL Service Management Practices
*   **Service Desk**: Single point of contact for users
*   **Incident Management**: Restore normal service operation quickly
*   **Problem Management**: Resolve underlying causes of incidents
*   **Change Enablement**: Respond to changing business needs
*   **Service Level Management**: Set clear service expectations
*   **Continual Improvement**: Align services with stakeholder needs

## 3. Four Dimensions of Service Management
*   **Organizations and People**: Roles, responsibilities, skills, competencies
*   **Information and Technology**: Information systems, knowledge bases, tools
*   **Partners and Suppliers**: Vendor relationships, contracts, supply chain
*   **Value Streams and Processes**: Workflows, procedures, activities

## 4. Incident Management
*   **Incident Logging**: Record all incidents with unique identifiers
*   **Categorization**: Classify incidents by type, impact, urgency
*   **Prioritization**: Determine incident priority based on impact and urgency
*   **Investigation and Diagnosis**: Identify root causes and solutions
*   **Resolution and Recovery**: Implement fixes and restore service
*   **Incident Closure**: Confirm resolution and close incident
*   **Communication**: Keep stakeholders informed throughout process

## 5. Problem Management
*   **Problem Identification**: Detect underlying causes of incidents
*   **Problem Logging**: Record problems with detailed information
*   **Investigation and Diagnosis**: Analyze root causes thoroughly
*   **Workaround Implementation**: Temporary solutions to reduce impact
*   **Permanent Solution**: Develop and implement lasting fixes
*   **Problem Closure**: Verify solution effectiveness and close problem

## 6. Change Management
*   **Change Evaluation**: Assess risk, impact, and benefits
*   **Change Authorization**: Obtain approval from appropriate authorities
*   **Change Implementation**: Execute approved changes with minimal disruption
*   **Change Review**: Evaluate change effectiveness and lessons learned
*   **Standard Changes**: Pre-authorized changes with established procedures
*   **Emergency Changes**: Expedited process for critical changes

## 7. Service Level Management
*   **SLA Definition**: Clear agreements on service quality, availability, performance
*   **Monitoring**: Continuous measurement of service performance
*   **Reporting**: Regular communication of service metrics to stakeholders
*   **Service Improvement**: Actions to enhance service quality
*   **Customer Satisfaction**: Regular assessment of customer experience

## 8. Continual Improvement
*   **Improvement Register**: Track all improvement opportunities
*   **Assessment**: Evaluate current state and identify gaps
*   **Analysis and Measurement**: Quantify improvement potential
*   **Presentation and Approval**: Communicate proposals and obtain approval
*   **Implementation**: Execute approved improvements
*   **Review and Close**: Evaluate results and close improvement initiatives

## 9. Monitoring and Event Management
*   **Event Detection**: Automated identification of system events
*   **Event Classification**: Categorize events by type and significance
*   **Event Prioritization**: Determine response urgency
*   **Event Response**: Appropriate actions based on event type
*   **Event Logging**: Maintain records of all significant events

## 10. Integration with Development
*   **DevOps Alignment**: Bridge development and operations practices
*   **Automation**: Automate routine operational tasks
*   **Collaboration**: Foster cooperation between teams
*   **Feedback Loops**: Rapid feedback for continuous improvement
*   **Measurement**: Key metrics for service and development performance
